Site Plan: Mountain Trekkers Club

Site Name

Mountain Trekkers Club

This name reflects a community focused on hiking adventures in mountainous regions. Optional domain: mountain-trekkers.org

Site Purpose

Provide trail guides, event schedules, gear recommendations, and membership registration for hiking enthusiasts.

Scenarios

  • What are the best beginner-friendly trails in the area?
  • How do I sign up for guided group hikes?

Color Schema

Primary Color (#2E5D2E): Headers, navigation bar.

Secondary Color (#F4E9D8): Background, card components.

Typography

Heading Font: 'Montserrat' (Bold) - Titles and section headers.

Body Font: 'Open Sans' - Paragraphs and general content.

Wireframe

Desktop View

Header with logo and navigation menu. Main content area divided into trail highlights, events calendar, and membership CTA.

Mobile View

Collapsible hamburger menu. Stacked content sections with simplified imagery.
